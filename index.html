Software Engineer | Designer | Volleyball Fanatic

I'm Justin—an engineer who builds, a designer who refines, and a volleyball fanatic who never turns down a good rally. I thrive on crafting intuitive digital experiences with clean, efficient design.

My work blends technical depth with creative precision. Whether it's developing seamless applications, refining modern UI/UX, or optimizing workflows, I focus on solutions that are both functional and aesthetic.

Outside of tech, you'll find me setting and spiking on the volleyball court, competing in pickleball matches, or experimenting with the perfect ratio of matcha to milk. I take my coffee, milk tea, and design choices seriously.

Originally from LA County (626), I have a deep appreciation for good food, well-crafted drinks, and timeless design. Seattle may be where I work, but my roots will always be in Southern California.
